Counseling and Psychotherapy

Counseling

Focuses on providing support and practical strategies for managing stress, relationships, and life changes.

Psychotherapy

Offers deeper, long-term treatment for issues like anxiety, depression, and trauma through evidence-based techniques.

Psychological Assessment and Diagnostics

Psychological Assessments

We use a variety of standardized tests and clinical interviews to evaluate cognitive, emotional, and behavioral functioning. Our thorough diagnostic evaluations help pinpoint specific mental health conditions, providing a clear understanding of your symptoms and their impact on your daily life.

Child and Adolescent Counseling

Child Counseling

Our child counseling services address issues like anxiety, depression, and behavioral problems, offering a safe space for children to express their feelings and learn coping skills.

Adolescent Counseling

We support teens dealing with stress, self-esteem issues, and mental health concerns. Our adolescent counseling provides a confidential environment to explore their emotions and develop healthy strategies.

IQ and Learning Disability Assessment

IQ Assessments

We use standardized intelligence tests. Understanding IQ can help identify strengths and areas for improvement, aiding in academic and career planning.

Learning Assessments

Our learning assessments identify specific learning disabilities, such as dyslexia, dyscalculia, and other challenges. These assessments help in creating effective educational plans tailored to child's needs.

Family and Couple Therapy

Family Therapy

Family therapy helps families navigate challenges and improve their relationships. Our therapists work with families to address issues such as communication problems, parenting conflicts, and life transitions.

Couple Therapy

Our couple therapy focuses on enhancing communication and resolving conflicts between partners. Whether you're facing specific issues or simply want to improve your relationship, we're here to help.

Learning Disability Management

Learning Disability

We provide specialized services to support individuals with learning disabilities. Our goal is to help children and adults overcome challenges and achieve their full potential. Based on the assessment results, we create tailored intervention plans and offer counseling and support for individuals and families to help them navigate the emotional and social impacts of learning disabilities.

Comprehensive Career Counselling by Expert
